Senior Software Engineer – Backend Platform

We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Backend Platform team. You will design, build, and operate distributed systems that power our core product APIs.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable backend services using Python and/or Node.js
- Architect RESTful and event-driven APIs consumed by web and mobile clients
- Own the reliability, performance, and observability of your services (SLOs, alerting, on-call)
- Collaborate with product and frontend teams to deliver new features end-to-end
- Review code, mentor junior engineers, and contribute to technical standards
- Drive data-modelling decisions for PostgreSQL and NoSQL data stores

Requirements:
- 4+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python or Node.js (both preferred)
- Experience building and operating production APIs (REST, GraphQL, or gRPC)
- Solid understanding of distributed systems: caching, queuing, eventual consistency
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with SQL (PostgreSQL preferred) and at least one NoSQL store (Redis, MongoDB, DynamoDB)
- Strong communication skills and comfort working in an async-first remote environment

Nice to have:
- Experience with RAG pipelines, vector databases, or LLM-integrated systems
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Pulumi)
